# Workflow
Prefer opening an issue before contributing non-trivial features. No issues needed for:
* Fixing obvious bugs;
* Fixing typos;
* Bumping dependency versions;
* Etc.

If your PR contains code that I deem to be a minor mistake or is good as-is, I will fix it myself and merge without waiting for a response.
Otherwise you may be expected to amend your patch to match my standards and expectations.

If you are not done, please submit as a draft -- I might offer a preliminary review for such.

# Conventions
Prefix your commits appropriately:
* `chore: ` for fixing clippy warnings or general cleanups with no change to behavior.
* `fix: ` for bugfixes.
* `feat: ` for new features. This includes adding new tests.

If you're on the edge, choose whichever feels best. Consider splitting your change into separate commits if applicable and feasible.

# Versioning
Follow [semver](https://semver.org/).
